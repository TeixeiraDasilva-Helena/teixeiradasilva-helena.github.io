About Me

I am pursuing a Master's in Aerospace and Mechanical Engineering with a concentration in AI and ML at USC, expected to graduate in May 2026. My research has focused on legged robotics and autonomous path planning for field operations, complemented by coursework in advanced control techniques and deep learning. My goal is to create intelligent robotic systems that deliver tangible impact in addressing real-world challenges.
